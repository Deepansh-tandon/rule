IPL AUCTION

1. Each participating team will have to acquire a ground, a franchise, and a minimum number of 14 players but maximum of 16players.
2. Teams will be allowed to bid at current price plus 2cr after the current crosses 10cr.
3. The above funds allotted are separate for each. That is, you are supposed to buy the ground and franchise within the 20 Cr. budget constraint. If the amount exceeds 20 Cr. for the purchase of both franchise and the ground, the exceeded amount will be doubled and deducted from the player fund as a penalty.
4. Initial budget: Rs80 cr for players +20 cr for Teams and Stadiums
